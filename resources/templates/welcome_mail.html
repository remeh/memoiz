Welcome to Memoiz!
Never forget a memo again.

Putting notes on post-its or in an application is easy. To forget them as soon as you wrote them is even easier. That is why Memoiz has been created: never let you write something down and forget about it.

Memoiz takes care of sending you smart emails and push notifications, with enriched context about your memos when possible.

Moreover, when you'll add your memos in Memoiz, our algorithm will try to automatically categorize them, helping you finding them easily.
